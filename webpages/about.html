About Us Posers

Poser was created for people who love fashion inspired by iconic celebrity style but want it to feel wearable in everyday life. We take influence from the looks that shape pop culture and turn them into pieces that are accessible, comfortable, and current. Our goal is to help you express confidence through outfits that feel bold without trying too hard. At Poser, style isn’t about copying someone else—it’s about finding the version of it that fits you.

Poser was built on one idea: the looks you see on celebrities shouldn’t be untouchable. We break down iconic outfits and rework them into pieces you can actually wear on a normal day. Fashion is influence, remix, and attitude, and Poser is where all three meet. You’re not pretending to be someone else; you’re owning the energy that made those looks unforgettable.

At Poser, we believe style is a form of self-expression. Inspired by the confidence and creativity seen in celebrity fashion, our collections are designed to help you step into your own spotlight. We turn inspiration into wearable pieces so you can experiment with your look and show up feeling like your best self. Because confidence isn’t about fame, it’s about how you carry yourself.

Poser is for people who know exactly where their style inspiration comes from, and aren’t pretending otherwise. We take celebrity looks, strip away the red carpet, and turn them into clothes you can actually wear outside your house. No stylists, no paparazzi, no private jets, just good outfits. Call it influence. Call it imitation. We call it getting dressed.

Let’s be honest: most fashion starts with someone famous wearing something cool. Poser just skips pretending. Our pieces are inspired by celebrity style, reworked for real life, and priced so you don’t need a record deal to afford them. You’re not famous. Neither are we. The outfit still works.

Celebrities wear cool stuff. People copy it. We just made it easier. Poser takes iconic looks and turns them into wearable pieces for normal life, no fame required. Because style travels fast, and pretending it doesn’t would be weird.
